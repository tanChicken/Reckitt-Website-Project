Safety & Warnings

Allergy Warning

Do not use if you are allergic to aspirin or any other NSAIDs.

Medical Conditions

Consult a doctor if you have stomach ulcers, kidney disease, or are pregnant.

Concomitant Use

Avoid taking other ibuprofen-containing products at the same time.
